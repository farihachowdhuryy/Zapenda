Sizing & Measurements

Every Zapenda piece is made-to-order. Choose a standard size (XS–3XL) or enter your exact measurements for a truly perfect fit.

📏

Standard Sizes

Choose from XS, S, M, L, XL, 2XL, or 3XL when adding any item to your cart.

📐

Custom Sizing

Select "Custom Sizing" when adding to cart and enter your exact measurements.

How to Measure

MeasurementHow to Take It
Bust / ChestMeasure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the ground.
WaistMeasure around your natural waistline — the narrowest part of your torso.
HipsMeasure around the fullest part of your hips, approximately 8 inches below the waist.
Shoulder WidthMeasure across your back from shoulder tip to shoulder tip.
InseamMeasure from the crotch to the floor along the inside of the leg.
Sleeve LengthMeasure from shoulder tip to wrist with your arm slightly bent.
HeightStand straight without shoes and measure from head to floor.
