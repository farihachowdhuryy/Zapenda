How to Measure Yourself

  • Use a soft, flexible measuring tape (not a metal ruler)
  • Measure over light clothing or undergarments — not bulky layers
  • Keep the tape snug but not tight — you should be able to slide a finger under it
  • Stand naturally and breathe normally while measuring
  • Have a friend help for back measurements
  • All Zapenda garments are made to your exact measurements — enter them when ordering for the best fit

Measurement Guide

📐

Bust / Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest. Keep tape parallel to the floor, arms relaxed.

⬜

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline — the narrowest part of your torso, usually 1 inch above the navel.

🔵

Hips

Around the fullest part of your hips and seat, about 7–9 inches below your natural waist.

📏

Height

Stand straight without shoes. Measure from the top of your head to the floor.

🦵

Inseam

From the crotch seam to the bottom of the leg along the inner seam. Best measured on a well-fitting pair of pants.

💪

Shoulder Width

Across your back from the outer edge of one shoulder seam to the other.
