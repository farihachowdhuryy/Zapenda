Custom Order FAQ

How long does a custom order take?

Custom orders typically take 6–10 weeks from order confirmation to delivery. We'll give you an estimated timeline after reviewing your request.

How much does a completely custom order cost?

Most custom pieces range from $100 to $600+. Our team will provide a quote after reviewing your request.

Can I book an in-person fitting in Detroit?

Yes! We offer private fitting appointments at our Detroit showroom for $25 (applied to your order). Contact us via WhatsApp or email to schedule.
