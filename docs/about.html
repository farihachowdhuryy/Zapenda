Our Story

Built on Craft.
Grounded in Congo.

From a Detroit kitchen table to artisan workshops in Bukavu — this is Zapenda.

Why We Exist

Zapenda was founded with a singular mission: create meaningful employment for skilled tailors in the Democratic Republic of Congo while bringing the richness of African craft to a global audience.

Every piece in our collection is made to order, crafted specifically for the person who ordered it. That means no waste, no mass production, and a direct connection between you and the artisan who made your garment.

✦

Artisan-Made

Every garment handcrafted by skilled tailors in Bukavu and Goma, DRC.

Community First

Detroit Roots

Though our manufacturing is in Congo, Zapenda's heart is in Detroit. We host private fitting appointments at our Detroit showroom — $25 (credited to your order).

The Drops

Zapenda releases collections in "Drops." Drop One introduced our flagship apparel and outerwear. Drop Two expanded into new silhouettes. Each drop is limited — when it's gone, it's gone.
