The Founder

Stella's Story

Zapenda was born from a love of African culture, Congolese craftsmanship, and a deep desire to create meaningful employment for talented tailors in the DRC.

Stella, our founder, grew up navigating two worlds — her Congolese heritage and her life in Detroit. Fashion became the bridge: a way to honor both, celebrate identity, and connect communities across continents.

The name Zapenda comes from Swahili, meaning love. Every garment we create is infused with that love — for the craft, the culture, the artisans, and our customers.

Why Congo?

Eastern Congo is home to some of the world's most talented textile artisans. By partnering with tailors in Bukavu and Goma, we're investing in a region that needs it most while delivering extraordinary quality to our customers worldwide.
