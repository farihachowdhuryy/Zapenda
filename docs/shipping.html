Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Shipping

Shipping will be calculated at checkout.

Made-to-Order Production Time

All Zapenda garments are made-to-order by our skilled tailors in Bukavu and Goma, eastern Congo. Please allow 4–6 weeks for production before your order is shipped. Fully custom garments (from our Custom Order program) may require 6–8 weeks depending on complexity.

International Shipping

We ship worldwide! International orders may be subject to customs duties and import taxes, which are the responsibility of the customer. Please check your country's import policies before ordering.

Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email so you can monitor your delivery.

Questions?

For shipping questions, please contact us at info@zapendashop.com or message us on WhatsApp.
