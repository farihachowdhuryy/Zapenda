January 22, 2023

New Year Update! 🎉

We started Zapenda with the mission to create employment opportunities for talented tailors in Congo. With the success of our holiday pop-up, we were able to hire two new staff members in Congo, contracted local Detroit talent for our marketing efforts, and were able to provide surgery for one of our Master Tailors.

This year, we are excited to continue growing and expanding our impact. We have new collections in the works, new partnerships being formed, and a commitment to making sure every person who works with Zapenda thrives.

Thank you for being part of this journey. Your support means the world to us — and to our tailors in Bukavu and Goma. Every purchase you make directly impacts families in eastern Congo.

In 2023, we're launching:

  • Academy Drop 2 — Swahili Shule, a full wearable fashion line
  • Private fitting sessions at our Detroit showroom
  • Custom order program — design your own garment with our tailors
  • Community events — pop-ups and cultural celebrations in Detroit

With love,
Stella & the Zapenda Team 💖
